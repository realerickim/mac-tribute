A Hip Hop Legend

Malcolm James McCormick (January 19, 1992 - September 7, 2018), also known as Mac Miller, was an American rapper. He grew up influenced by fellow Pittsburgh artist Wiz Khalifa, and signed a deal with his hometown record label, Rostrum Records, in 2010. His first album, Blue Slide Park, hit the #1 spot on US Billboard 200. In 2013, he created his own record label, REMember Music, in honour of one of his friends that died. His subsequent albums, GO:OD AM (2015), The Divine Feminine (2016), and Swimming (2018) were huge succeses. He passed away from drug overdose on September 7, 2018.
